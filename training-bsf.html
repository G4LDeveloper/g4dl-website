About BSF Farming

Black Soldier Fly (BSF) larvae are a highly efficient source of protein for animal feed and composting organic waste. Our training teaches sustainable, low-cost techniques to produce BSF for income and environmental benefits.

What You'll Learn

Waste Management

Convert organic waste into high-quality larvae for animal feed and biofertilizers, reducing environmental impact.

Protein Production

Harvest nutrient-rich BSF larvae for poultry, fish, and livestock feed.

Organic Fertilizer

Produce compost from BSF frass, creating a sustainable, organic fertilizer for farms and gardens.

Entrepreneurship

Learn to set up a small-scale BSF farm and generate income sustainably.
