Digital Literacy Training

Build practical digital skills for work, entrepreneurship and community development — from basic computer use to online safety and digital tools for agribusiness.

Join a Cohort

About this program

Our Digital Literacy program equips learners with practical, hands-on knowledge they can apply immediately. The course is beginner-friendly and designed with rural and urban participants in mind.

Learning outcomes
  • Use a computer, mobile device and common office applications.
  • Access online markets, banking and digital tools for small businesses.
  • Practice strong password hygiene, identify phishing and stay safer online.
  • Use social media and messaging responsibly for community engagement and marketing.

Course structure & schedule

Format

Blended — in-person workshops + guided remote sessions. Practical labs every week.

Duration

6 weeks (2 sessions per week, 3 hours each) or an accelerated 3-week cohort (weekend intensive).

Modules
  • Intro to devices & operating systems
  • Internet fundamentals & online safety
  • Productivity tools: docs, sheets, slides
  • Communication: email, messaging, scheduling
  • Digital marketing basics & selling online
  • Using phones for farm data & market access
Who should apply?

Youth, farmers, women entrepreneurs, community group leaders and anyone who wants to use digital tools to grow livelihoods.

Module highlights

Hands-on lessons with practical outcomes — you will leave with a real plan to use digital tools for income/efficiency.

  • Create and share documents, track simple budgets in a spreadsheet.
  • Set up an email, use calendar invites and maintain contacts.
  • Capture quality photos and list products on marketplaces.
  • Protect your accounts: passwords, 2FA, spotting scams.
  • Intro to simple digital bookkeeping and invoicing.

Support & devices

We provide training devices during in-person sessions when needed. We also help learners set up low-cost or free tools that run on basic smartphones.

Cost & scholarships

Course fees are kept affordable. Scholarships and group discounts available — contact the team for details.
