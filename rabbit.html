About This Training Program

Our Rabbit Farming Training is designed to equip you with essential skills for starting and managing a profitable rabbit farm. Learn about breeding, feeding, housing, and marketing to turn rabbit farming into a sustainable business.

What You'll Learn

  • Introduction to rabbit breeds and market potential
  • Setting up rabbit housing and environment
  • Feeding and nutrition management
  • Breeding techniques and record keeping
  • Disease prevention and healthcare
  • Harvesting and selling strategies
  • Business planning and financial management
