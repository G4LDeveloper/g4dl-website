Our Programs

We have made significant strides in empowering youth for sustainable development across Kenya and beyond. We have engaged with diverse communities, facilitated impactful trainings, and fostered a generation of young leaders committed to achieving food security and economic independence.

Urban Farming Training

Our flagship program equips youth with hands-on skills in sustainable urban farming. Participants learn container gardening, vertical farming, mushroom cultivation, and how to transform underutilized spaces into productive farms. Through partnerships with rehabilitation centers, schools, and community organizations, we've trained hundreds of young people in practical agriculture that generates income and promotes food security.

Digital & AI Literacy for Agripreneurs

This innovative program combines agricultural knowledge with digital skills and AI literacy. Young farmers learn to use technology for farm management, market access, data analysis, and business development. Through this program, we're building a generation of tech-savvy agripreneurs who bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern innovation.

Agricultural Value Addition & Entrepreneurship

We teach communities how to add value to agricultural products, increasing income and market opportunities. From processing and packaging to branding and marketing, participants learn the full value chain of agricultural entrepreneurship. Success stories include youth-led startups in mushroom farming, rabbit keeping, vermicomposting, and organic produce.

Inclusive Farming for All

Grow4Liberty is committed to creating opportunities for everyone, including women and persons with disabilities. Our programs are designed to be accessible and inclusive, ensuring that all community members can participate, learn, and thrive. We partner with organizations serving marginalized groups to deliver tailored training that promotes dignity, independence, and economic empowerment.
